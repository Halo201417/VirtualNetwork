#1
We ensure that the network is connected
h1 ping -c 50 h2

#2
Now we are going to inject traffic:
h1 sysctl -w net.ipv4.tcp_congestion_control=reno
h2 sysctl -w net.ipv4.tcp_congestion_control=reno

We enable the iperf server
h2 iperf3 -s &

And we start capturing with wireshark
h1 tcpdump -i h1-eth0 -w reno.pcap &

We generate some traffic
h1 iperf3 -c 10.0.2.2 -t 20

Now we stop the packet capture
h1 killall tcpdump
